"id": ".\AN5\AN5.118", "series": ".\AN5\AN5.118", "text": "Aṅguttara Nikāya 5.118 ,12. Andhakavindavagga ,Micchādiṭṭhikasutta ,“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgatā bhikkhunī yathābhataṁ nikkhittā evaṁ niraye. ,Katamehi pañcahi? ,Ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā avaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, ,ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā vaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, ,micchādiṭṭhikā ca hoti, ,micchāsaṅkappā ca, ,saddhādeyyaṁ vinipāteti. ,Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgatā bhikkhunī yathābhataṁ nikkhittā evaṁ niraye. ,Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgatā bhikkhunī yathābhataṁ nikkhittā evaṁ sagge. ,Katamehi pañcahi? ,Anuvicca pariyogāhetvā avaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, ,anuvicca pariyogāhetvā vaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaṁ bhāsati, ,sammādiṭṭhikā ca hoti, ,sammāsaṅkappā ca, ,saddhādeyyaṁ na vinipāteti. ,Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgatā bhikkhunī yathābhataṁ nikkhittā evaṁ sagge”ti. ,Aṭṭhamaṁ.
