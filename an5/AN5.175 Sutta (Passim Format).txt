Upāsakavagga ,Caṇḍālasutta ,“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato upāsako upāsakacaṇḍālo ca hoti upāsakamalañca upāsakapatikuṭṭho ca. ,Katamehi pañcahi? ,Assaddho hoti; ,dussīlo hoti; ,kotūhalamaṅgaliko hoti, maṅgalaṁ pacceti no kammaṁ; ,ito ca bahiddhā dakkhiṇeyyaṁ gavesati; ,tattha ca pubbakāraṁ karoti. ,Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato upāsako upāsakacaṇḍālo ca hoti upāsakamalañca upāsakapatikuṭṭho ca. ,Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato upāsako upāsakaratanañca hoti upāsakapadumañca upāsakapuṇḍarīkañca. ,Katamehi pañcahi? ,Saddho hoti; ,sīlavā hoti; ,akotūhalamaṅgaliko hoti, kammaṁ pacceti no maṅgalaṁ; ,na ito bahiddhā dakkhiṇeyyaṁ gavesati; ,idha ca pubbakāraṁ karoti. ,Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato upāsako upāsakaratanañca hoti upāsakapadumañca upāsakapuṇḍarīkañcā”ti. ,Pañcamaṁ.
