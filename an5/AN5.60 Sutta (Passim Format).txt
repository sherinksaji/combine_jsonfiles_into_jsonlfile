Nīvaraṇavagga ,Dutiyavuḍḍhapabbajitasutta ,Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato dullabho vuḍḍhapabbajito. ,Katamehi pañcahi? ,Dullabho, bhikkhave, vuḍḍhapabbajito suvaco, dullabho suggahitaggāhī, dullabho padakkhiṇaggāhī, dullabho dhammakathiko, dullabho vinayadharo. ,Imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato dullabho vuḍḍhapabbajito”ti. ,Dasamaṁ. ,Nīvaraṇavaggo paṭhamo. ,Tassuddānaṁ ,Āvaraṇaṁ rāsi aṅgāni, ,samayaṁ mātuputtikā; ,Upajjhā ṭhānā licchavi, ,kumārā aparā duveti.
