Dīghacārikavagga ,Paṭhamakaṇhasappasutta ,“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā kaṇhasappe. ,Katame pañca? ,Asuci, duggandho, sabhīru, sappaṭibhayo, mittadubbhī. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā kaṇhasappe. ,Evamevaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcime ādīnavā mātugāme. ,Katame pañca? ,Asuci, duggandho, sabhīru, sappaṭibhayo, mittadubbhī. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā mātugāme”ti. ,Navamaṁ.
