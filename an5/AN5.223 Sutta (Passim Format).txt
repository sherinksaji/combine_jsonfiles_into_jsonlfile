Dīghacārikavagga ,Atinivāsasutta ,“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā atinivāse. ,Katame pañca? ,Bahubhaṇḍo hoti bahubhaṇḍasannicayo, bahubhesajjo hoti bahubhesajjasannicayo, bahukicco hoti bahukaraṇīyo byatto kiṅkaraṇīyesu, saṁsaṭṭho viharati gahaṭṭhapabbajitehi ananulomikena gihisaṁsaggena, tamhā ca āvāsā pakkamanto sāpekkho pakkamati. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā atinivāse. ,Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṁsā samavatthavāse. ,Katame pañca? ,Na bahubhaṇḍo hoti na bahubhaṇḍasannicayo, na bahubhesajjo hoti na bahubhesajjasannicayo, na bahukicco hoti na bahukaraṇīyo na byatto kiṅkaraṇīyesu, asaṁsaṭṭho viharati gahaṭṭhapabbajitehi ananulomikena gihisaṁsaggena, tamhā ca āvāsā pakkamanto anapekkho pakkamati. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṁsā samavatthavāse”ti. ,Tatiyaṁ.
