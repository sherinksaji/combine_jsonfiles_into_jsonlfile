Gilānavagga ,Dutiyaanāyussāsutta ,“Pañcime, bhikkhave, dhammā anāyussā. ,Katame pañca? ,Asappāyakārī hoti, sappāye mattaṁ na jānāti, apariṇatabhojī ca hoti, dussīlo ca, pāpamitto ca. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca dhammā anāyussā. ,Pañcime, bhikkhave, dhammā āyussā. ,Katame pañca? ,Sappāyakārī hoti, sappāye mattaṁ jānāti, pariṇatabhojī ca hoti, sīlavā ca, kalyāṇamitto ca. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca dhammā āyussā”ti. ,Chaṭṭhaṁ.
