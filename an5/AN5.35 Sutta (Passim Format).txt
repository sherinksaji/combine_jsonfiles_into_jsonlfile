Sumanavagga ,Dānānisaṁsasutta ,“Pañcime, bhikkhave, dāne ānisaṁsā. ,Katame pañca? ,Bahuno janassa piyo hoti manāpo; ,santo sappurisā bhajanti; ,kalyāṇo kittisaddo abbhuggacchati; ,gihidhammā anapagato hoti; ,kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā sugatiṁ saggaṁ lokaṁ upapajjati. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca dāne ānisaṁsāti. ,Dadamāno piyo hoti, ,sataṁ dhammaṁ anukkamaṁ; ,Santo naṁ sadā bhajanti, ,saññatā brahmacārayo. ,Te tassa dhammaṁ desenti, ,Sabbadukkhāpanūdanaṁ; ,Yaṁ so dhammaṁ idhaññāya, ,Parinibbāti anāsavo”ti. ,Pañcamaṁ.
