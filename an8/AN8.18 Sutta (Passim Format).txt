Mahāvagga ,Dutiyabandhanasutta ,“Aṭṭhahi, bhikkhave, ākārehi puriso itthiṁ bandhati. ,Katamehi aṭṭhahi? ,Ruṇṇena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ,hasitena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ,bhaṇitena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ,ākappena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ,vanabhaṅgena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ,gandhena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ,rasena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati; ,phassena, bhikkhave, puriso itthiṁ bandhati. ,Imehi kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭhahākārehi puriso itthiṁ bandhati. ,Te, bhikkhave, sattā subaddhā, ye phassena baddhā”ti. ,Aṭṭhamaṁ.
