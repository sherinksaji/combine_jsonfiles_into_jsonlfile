"id": ".\AN10\AN10.111", "series": ".\AN10\AN10.111", "text": "Aṅguttara Nikāya 10.111 ,11. Samaṇasaññāvagga ,Paṭhamaasekhasutta ,Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ nisīdi. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca: ,“‘Asekho asekho’ti, bhante, vuccati. ,Kittāvatā bhante, bhikkhu asekho hotī”ti? ,“Idha, bhikkhu, bhikkhu asekhāya sammādiṭṭhiyā samannāgato hoti, asekhena sammāsaṅkappena samannāgato hoti, asekhāya sammāvācāya samannāgato hoti, asekhena sammākammantena samannāgato hoti, asekhena sammāājīvena samannāgato hoti, asekhena sammāvāyāmena samannāgato hoti, asekhāya sammāsatiyā samannāgato hoti, asekhena sammāsamādhinā samannāgato hoti, asekhena sammāñāṇena samannāgato hoti, asekhāya sammāvimuttiyā samannāgato hoti. ,Evaṁ kho, bhikkhu, bhikkhu asekho hotī”ti. ,Ekādasamaṁ.
