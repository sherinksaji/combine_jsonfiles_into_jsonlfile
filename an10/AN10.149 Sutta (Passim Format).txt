Ariyavagga ,Uppādetabbasutta ,“Uppādetabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na uppādetabbañca. ,Taṁ suṇātha …pe… ,katamo ca, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo? ,Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… micchāvimutti—,ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo. ,Katamo ca, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo? ,Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… sammāvimutti—,ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo”ti. ,Pañcamaṁ.
