Sādhuvagga ,Dukkhavipākasutta ,“Dukkhavipākañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi sukhavipākañca. ,Taṁ suṇātha …pe… ,katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhavipāko dhammo? ,Micchādiṭṭhi …pe… micchāvimutti—,ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dukkhavipāko dhammo. ,Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukhavipāko dhammo? ,Sammādiṭṭhi …pe… sammāvimutti—,ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukhavipāko dhammo”ti. ,Ekādasamaṁ. ,Sādhuvaggo catuttho.
