Vajjisattakavagga ,Saññāsutta ,“Satta vo, bhikkhave, aparihāniye dhamme desessāmi. ,Taṁ suṇātha, sādhukaṁ manasi karotha …pe…. ,Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta aparihāniyā dhammā? ,Yāvakīvañca, bhikkhave, bhikkhū aniccasaññaṁ bhāvessanti; vuddhiyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhūnaṁ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. ,Yāvakīvañca, bhikkhave, bhikkhū anattasaññaṁ bhāvessanti …pe… ,asubhasaññaṁ bhāvessanti … ,ādīnavasaññaṁ bhāvessanti … ,pahānasaññaṁ bhāvessanti … ,virāgasaññaṁ bhāvessanti … ,nirodhasaññaṁ bhāvessanti; ,vuddhiyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhūnaṁ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. Yāvakīvañca, bhikkhave, ime satta aparihāniyā dhammā bhikkhūsu ṭhassanti, imesu ca sattasu aparihāniyesu dhammesu, bhikkhū sandississanti; vuddhiyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhūnaṁ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihānī”ti. ,Sattamaṁ.
