Dhanavagga ,Uggasutta ,Atha kho uggo rājamahāmatto yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ nisīdi. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho uggo rājamahāmatto bhagavantaṁ etadavoca: ,“Acchariyaṁ, bhante, abbhutaṁ, bhante. ,Yāva aḍḍho cāyaṁ, bhante, migāro rohaṇeyyo yāva mahaddhano yāva mahābhogo”ti. ,“Kīva aḍḍho panugga, migāro rohaṇeyyo, kīva mahaddhano, kīva mahābhogo”ti? ,“Sataṁ, bhante, satasahassānaṁ hiraññassa, ko pana vādo rūpiyassā”ti. ,“Atthi kho etaṁ, ugga, dhanaṁ netaṁ ‘natthī’ti vadāmīti. ,Tañca kho etaṁ, ugga, dhanaṁ sādhāraṇaṁ agginā udakena rājūhi corehi appiyehi dāyādehi. ,Satta kho imāni, ugga, dhanāni asādhāraṇāni agginā udakena rājūhi corehi appiyehi dāyādehi. ,Katamāni satta? ,Saddhādhanaṁ, sīladhanaṁ, hirīdhanaṁ, ottappadhanaṁ, sutadhanaṁ, cāgadhanaṁ, paññādhanaṁ. ,Imāni kho, ugga, satta dhanāni asādhāraṇāni agginā udakena rājūhi corehi appiyehi dāyādehīti. ,Saddhādhanaṁ sīladhanaṁ, ,hirī ottappiyaṁ dhanaṁ; ,Sutadhanañca cāgo ca, ,paññā ve sattamaṁ dhanaṁ. ,Yassa ete dhanā atthi, ,itthiyā purisassa vā; ,Sa ve mahaddhano loke, ,ajeyyo devamānuse. ,Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca, ,pasādaṁ dhammadassanaṁ; ,Anuyuñjetha medhāvī, ,saraṁ buddhāna sāsanan”ti. ,Sattamaṁ.
