Dhanavagga ,Saṅkhittadhanasutta ,“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, dhanāni. ,Katamāni satta? ,Saddhādhanaṁ, sīladhanaṁ, hirīdhanaṁ, ottappadhanaṁ, sutadhanaṁ, cāgadhanaṁ, paññādhanaṁ. ,Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta dhanānīti. ,Saddhādhanaṁ sīladhanaṁ, ,hirī ottappiyaṁ dhanaṁ; ,Sutadhanañca cāgo ca, ,paññā ve sattamaṁ dhanaṁ. ,Yassa ete dhanā atthi, ,itthiyā purisassa vā; ,Adaliddoti taṁ āhu, ,amoghaṁ tassa jīvitaṁ. ,Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca, ,Pasādaṁ dhammadassanaṁ; ,Anuyuñjetha medhāvī, ,Saraṁ buddhāna sāsanan”ti. ,Pañcamaṁ.
