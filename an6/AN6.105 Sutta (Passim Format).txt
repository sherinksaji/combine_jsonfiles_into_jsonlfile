Ānisaṁsavagga ,Bhavasutta ,“Tayome, bhikkhave, bhavā pahātabbā, tīsu sikkhāsu sikkhitabbaṁ. ,Katame tayo bhavā pahātabbā? ,Kāmabhavo, rūpabhavo, arūpabhavo—,ime tayo bhavā pahātabbā. ,Katamāsu tīsu sikkhāsu sikkhitabbaṁ? ,Adhisīlasikkhāya, adhicittasikkhāya, adhipaññāsikkhāya—,imāsu tīsu sikkhāsu sikkhitabbaṁ. ,Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ime tayo bhavā pahīnā honti, imāsu ca tīsu sikkhāsu sikkhitasikkho hoti—,ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, bhikkhu acchecchi taṇhaṁ, vivattayi saṁyojanaṁ, sammā mānābhisamayā antamakāsi dukkhassā”ti. ,Dasamaṁ.
