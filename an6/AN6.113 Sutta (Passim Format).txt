Tikavagga ,Aratisutta ,“Tayome, bhikkhave, dhammā. ,Katame tayo? ,Arati, vihiṁsā, adhammacariyā. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo dhammā. ,Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ dhammānaṁ pahānāya tayo dhammā bhāvetabbā. ,Katame tayo? ,Aratiyā pahānāya muditā bhāvetabbā, vihiṁsāya pahānāya avihiṁsā bhāvetabbā, adhammacariyāya pahānāya dhammacariyā bhāvetabbā. ,Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ dhammānaṁ pahānāya ime tayo dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti. ,Sattamaṁ.
