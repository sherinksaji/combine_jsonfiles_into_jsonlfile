Anuttariyavagga ,Aparihāniyasutta ,“Chayime, bhikkhave, aparihāniye dhamme desessāmi, taṁ suṇātha …pe… ,katame ca, bhikkhave, cha aparihāniyā dhammā? ,Na kammārāmatā, na bhassārāmatā, na niddārāmatā, na saṅgaṇikārāmatā, sovacassatā, kalyāṇamittatā—,ime kho, bhikkhave, cha aparihāniyā dhammā. ,Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, atītamaddhānaṁ na parihāyiṁsu kusalehi dhammehi, sabbete imeheva chahi dhammehi na parihāyiṁsu kusalehi dhammehi. ,Yepi hi keci, bhikkhave, anāgatamaddhānaṁ na parihāyissanti kusalehi dhammehi, sabbete imeheva chahi dhammehi na parihāyissanti kusalehi dhammehi. ,Yepi hi keci, bhikkhave, etarahi na parihāyanti kusalehi dhammehi, sabbete imeheva chahi dhammehi na parihāyanti kusalehi dhammehī”ti. ,Dutiyaṁ.
