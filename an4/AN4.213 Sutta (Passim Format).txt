Parisāvagga ,Akataññutāsutta ,“Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye. ,Katamehi catūhi? ,Kāyaduccaritena, vacīduccaritena, manoduccaritena, akataññutā akataveditā—,imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye. ,Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge. ,Katamehi catūhi? ,Kāyasucaritena, vacīsucaritena, manosucaritena, kataññutā kataveditā—,imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge”ti. ,Tatiyaṁ.
