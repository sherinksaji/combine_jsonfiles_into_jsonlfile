Kammavagga ,Sappurisānisaṁsasutta ,“Sappurisaṁ, bhikkhave, nissāya cattāro ānisaṁsā pāṭikaṅkhā. ,Katame cattāro? ,Ariyena sīlena vaḍḍhati, ariyena samādhinā vaḍḍhati, ariyāya paññāya vaḍḍhati, ariyāya vimuttiyā vaḍḍhati—,sappurisaṁ, bhikkhave, nissāya ime cattāro ānisaṁsā pāṭikaṅkhā”ti. ,Ekādasamaṁ. ,Kammavaggo catuttho. ,Tassuddānaṁ ,Saṅkhitta vitthāra soṇakāyana, ,Sikkhāpadaṁ ariyamaggo bojjhaṅgaṁ; ,Sāvajjañceva abyābajjhaṁ, ,Samaṇo ca sappurisānisaṁsoti.
