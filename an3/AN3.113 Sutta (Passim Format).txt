Āpāyikavagga ,Āpāyikasutta ,“Tayome, bhikkhave, āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāya. ,Katame tayo? ,Yo ca abrahmacārī brahmacāripaṭiñño, ,yo ca suddhaṁ brahmacariyaṁ carantaṁ amūlakena abrahmacariyena anuddhaṁseti, ,yo cāyaṁ evaṁvādī evaṁdiṭṭhi: ‘natthi kāmesu doso’ti, so tāya kāmesu pātabyataṁ āpajjati. ,Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāyā”ti. ,Paṭhamaṁ.
