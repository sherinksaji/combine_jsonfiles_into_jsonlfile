Cūḷavagga ,Saṅkhatalakkhaṇasutta ,“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, saṅkhatassa saṅkhatalakkhaṇāni. ,Katamāni tīṇi? ,Uppādo paññāyati, vayo paññāyati, ṭhitassa aññathattaṁ paññāyati. ,Imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi saṅkhatassa saṅkhatalakkhaṇānī”ti. ,Sattamaṁ. ,Asaṅkhatalakkhaṇasutta ,“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatassa asaṅkhatalakkhaṇāni. ,Katamāni tīṇi? ,Na uppādo paññāyati, na vayo paññāyati, na ṭhitassa aññathattaṁ paññāyati. ,Imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi asaṅkhatassa asaṅkhatalakkhaṇānī”ti. ,Aṭṭhamaṁ.
