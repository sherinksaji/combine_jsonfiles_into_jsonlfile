Āpāyikavagga ,Dullabhasutta ,“Tiṇṇaṁ, bhikkhave, pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmiṁ. ,Katamesaṁ tiṇṇaṁ? ,Tathāgatassa, bhikkhave, arahato sammāsambuddhassa pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmiṁ, tathāgatappaveditassa dhammavinayassa desetā puggalo dullabho lokasmiṁ, kataññū katavedī puggalo dullabho lokasmiṁ. ,Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṁ pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmin”ti. ,Dutiyaṁ.
